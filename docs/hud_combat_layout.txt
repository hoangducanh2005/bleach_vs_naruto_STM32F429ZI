HUD COMBAT LAYOUT - VIZARD DEMO
Cap nhat: sau khi chot man combat muot va HUD moi.

Man hinh:
- LCD ngang 320x240.
- HUD nam tren cung, cao 37 px: y = 0..36.
- Vung combat gameplay bat dau tu sau HUD, nen don gian: troi + mat dat/duong.

Mau chinh trong Core/Src/battle_demo.c:
- RGB565_HUD_BG    = 0x0842
- RGB565_HUD_FRAME = 0xC618
- RGB565_BLACK     = 0x0000
- RGB565_WHITE     = 0xFFFF
- RGB565_HP_BACK   = 0x4208
- RGB565_HP_GOOD   = 0x07E0
- RGB565_HP_WARN   = 0xFD20
- RGB565_MANA      = 0x04FF
- RGB565_MANA_BACK = 0x18C6
- RGB565_ACCENT_CYAN   = 0x07FF
- RGB565_ACCENT_ORANGE = 0xFD20

Nen HUD:
- Fill full width: x=0, y=0, w=320, h=37, color RGB565_HUD_BG.
- Duong ke duoi HUD: x=0, y=36, w=320, h=1, color RGB565_HUD_FRAME.

Label nhan vat:
- Player label: "VIZARD", FONT1, x=10, y=3, text RGB565_WHITE, bg RGB565_HUD_BG.
- CPU label: "SASUKE", FONT1, x=262, y=3, text RGB565_WHITE, bg RGB565_HUD_BG.

Player meters:
- HP: x=10, y=15, w=104, h=8, reverse=0.
- HP border: RGB565_ACCENT_CYAN.
- Mana: x=10, y=27, w=84, h=5, reverse=0.
- Mana hien tai full 100% vi chua co logic mana rieng.

CPU meters:
- HP: x=206, y=15, w=104, h=8, reverse=1.
- HP border: RGB565_ACCENT_ORANGE.
- Mana: x=226, y=27, w=84, h=5, reverse=1.
- Reverse=1 de thanh do tu phai sang trai, nhin can doi voi player.

Timer giua:
- Luon hien thi "60".
- Khung timer: hollow rect x=142, y=5 den x=177, y=27, color RGB565_HUD_FRAME.
- Nen timer: fill x=143, y=6, w=34, h=21, color RGB565_BLACK.
- Text timer: "60", FONT3, x=148, y=8, text RGB565_WHITE, bg RGB565_BLACK.

Ham lien quan:
- Battle_DrawHud(): ve toan bo HUD luc init/full redraw.
- Battle_DrawHealthBar(x, y, hp, borderColor, reverse): ve HP.
- Battle_DrawManaBar(x, y, reverse): ve mana full tam thoi.
- Battle_DrawMeter(...): ham chung ve meter co reverse.

Cap nhat runtime:
- Khi player HP doi: Battle_DrawHealthBar(10U, 15U, s_player.hp, RGB565_ACCENT_CYAN, 0U).
- Khi CPU HP doi: Battle_DrawHealthBar(206U, 15U, s_cpu.hp, RGB565_ACCENT_ORANGE, 1U).
- Timer hien tai la static 60s, chua co countdown logic.

Ghi chu de ghep HUD sau nay:
- HUD nay dang uu tien gon, it che man combat, khong hien text huong dan nut.
- Neu them mana logic that, chi can thay value trong Battle_DrawManaBar/Battle_DrawMeter.
- Neu them countdown, cap nhat rieng vung timer x=142..177, y=5..27 de khong redraw ca HUD.
- Nen giu HUD cao khoang 37 px de tranh anh huong sprite va gameplay da can lai.
